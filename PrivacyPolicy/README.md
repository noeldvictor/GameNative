# Privacy Policy for GameThor
<sub>Last Updated: May 10, 2026</sub>

GameThor is a personal, vibecoded fork of GameNative customized for the AYN Thor handheld. It helps users access and launch legally owned games through supported store integrations and local containers.

## Data Collection and Usage

**Local Storage Only**
All sensitive user data, including Steam login credentials and session information, is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

**Store Integrations**
When you connect a supported store account, your device communicates directly with that store's servers to authenticate your account and access your game library. Any data collection, storage, or processing by those services is governed by their own privacy policies and terms of service.

**No In-App Analytics, Ads, or Recommendation Tracking**
GameThor removes the upstream analytics hooks, advertising/support prompts, affiliate-style recommendation data, and built-in recommendation feed. The app does not collect or transmit in-app usage analytics such as:

- Game launch and close events
- Selected configuration settings
- Hardware category information such as GPU family or driver type
- Performance indicators such as FPS ranges or crash flags

GameThor does not build user profiles, track browsing activity, sell data to third parties, show in-app ads, or send recommendation click/conversion data.

## Third-Party Services

**Store Platforms**
GameThor functions as an interface to supported store services and local runtime components. We have no control over or responsibility for how external services collect, use, or store your information. Please refer to each connected service's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

## Data Security

**Local Protection**
Your login credentials and session data are stored securely on your device using Android's standard security protocols. This data is only used to facilitate communication between your device and Steam's servers.

## User Rights

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by:
- Logging out of the app
- Clearing app data through Android settings
- Uninstalling the application

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy within the app.

## Contact Information

This is a personal experimental fork with no formal support channel.

## Age Requirements

Users must satisfy the age requirements for any store account or service they connect.
